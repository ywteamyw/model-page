Riva 112’ Dolcevita Super

Built to Go Further

The Riva 112’ Dolcevita Super is the next evolution of the 110’ Dolcevita and one of the most refined flybridge yachts in her class. Expanded volumes, sculpted Italian lines, and twin MTU 16V 2000 M96L engines give her the presence of a true superyacht. On board, generous outdoor spaces, panoramic interiors, and a five-cabin layout create a yacht built around open-air living and a closer connection to the water. From the sun deck to the redesigned aft areas, every space is shaped for effortless entertaining, extended cruising, and the unmistakable elegance of Riva.
